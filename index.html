About The Survey

The Nevada School Survey is how you see your school in your own way. There will be 3 sections in this Survey which are the Staff, Middle School, and High School. Anyone could take the survey if you are a Staff Member, Middle Schoolers, or High Schoolers.

If you are not a student or don't know anything about any school, you can still take this survey if you like.
